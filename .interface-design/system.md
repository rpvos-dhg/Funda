# Interface Design System — Funda Shortlist

Persisted design decisions for the daily Funda apartment-hunting report/PWA.
Reuse these values; don't reinvent them.

## Intent
- **Who:** a first-time buyer (starter) hunting an owner-occupied apartment in the
  Den Haag region on a tight budget. Checks the report twice a day on an iPhone
  (installed PWA), usually while commuting.
- **What:** triage today's supply fast — spot what is NEW since this morning, judge
  in ~3 seconds whether the monthly cost fits the personal norm, then open on Funda
  or dismiss. Secondary: price drops + long-on-market listings (negotiation leverage),
  commute distance, map.
- **Feel:** a broker's calm morning **property dossier** — financial, trustworthy,
  decisive. Not a flashy listings portal.

## Direction: "Makelaarsdossier"
Den Haag residential property. Warm sandstone paper surfaces, ink text, and **The
Hague's heraldic green** as the single brand/identity accent.

## Tokens (defined in `:root`, see `HTML_CSS` in funda_rapport.py)
- **Surfaces:** `--paper #f3efe6` (app bg) · `--surface #fbf9f3` (cards) ·
  `--surface-2 #f0ebdf` (insets, slightly darker than surroundings).
- **Lines:** `--line #e3ddcd` · `--line-strong #d3cbb6`. Depth is **borders-first**.
- **Ink (4 levels):** `--ink #22271d` · `--ink-2 #4d5244` · `--muted #6b6b5c` ·
  `--faint #9d9a89`.
- **Brand:** `--brand #1f6b4a` · `--brand-deep #154c34` · `--brand-tint #e6efe6`.
- **Semantic status (state only, never decoration):** good = green family ·
  warn = `--warn #9a5a23` (amber) · alert = `--alert #9c3724` (brick red) ·
  gold = `--gold #b3842e` (NHG/notary, rare). Each has a `-tint` (light bg) and
  `-ink` (accessible dark text). All text/tint pairs verified ≥ 4.5:1 (WCAG AA).
- **Radius scale (concentric):** `--r-xs 5` (badges/inputs) · `--r-sm 7` (buttons/
  insets) · `--r-md 10` (cards/panels) · `--r-lg 14` (overlays/lock/popups).
- **Elevation (overlays only):** `--shadow-overlay`, `--shadow-pop`. Flat cards
  otherwise — no resting drop shadows.

## Depth strategy — ONE: borders-first
Cards, panels, profile, assumptions = 1px `--line` borders, no shadow at rest.
Shadow appears only on true overlays (sticky topbar, Leaflet popups, lock screen)
and on card `:hover`.

## Typography
- **Display/headings** (`h1, h2, h3`, brand wordmark, card title, prices, maandlast
  total): serif stack `--font-serif` (Iowan/Palatino/Georgia). This is the dossier
  signature — passes the swap test vs. a generic sans UI.
- **Data/labels/body:** system sans `--font-sans`.
- **All numbers:** `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`.
- **Type scale ≈ 1.25:** caption 11 · body 13–14 · 15–16 · h2 23 · h1 clamp(26–35).

## Spacing & density
Base unit 4px. Card padding 16px (comfortable triage). Uneven rhythm: tight within
groups, larger gaps between sections (`.section-row` margin 34px top).

## Signature: the affordability spine
Every listing card carries a 4px left border colored by budget fit
(`.card.budget-PAST` green · `.budget-KRAP`/`.budget-NORM` amber · `.budget-MAX`
red). It is the instant, scannable read of the product's whole reason to exist —
"can I afford this?" The maandlast total is the card's focal data point (serif,
tabular, larger).

## Number formatting
Dutch convention: thousands separated by a period via `_nl_getal()`
(e.g. `€ 289.000`, not `€ 289,000`). Use `_money()` / `_fmt_eur()` for display.

## Localization
All UI copy is Dutch. Keep it that way.

## States & accessibility (mandatory)
- `:focus-visible` ring (`--brand`, 2px, offset 2px) globally and on the lock input.
- Hover + `:active { transform: scale(.97–.98) }` press feedback on buttons/links.
- Touch targets ≥ 40px (Leaflet popup actions bumped to 40px).
- Informative `alt` text on listing photos.
- `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` neutralizes transitions/animations.

## Motion
Durations < 200ms, ease `cubic-bezier(0.23, 1, 0.32, 1)`. Animate transform/opacity
only. Reduced-motion respected.

## Brand assets
App icon = deep-green ground, notary-gold map-pin/teardrop, paper house glyph.
PNG icons generated by `_write_png_icon`; SVGs in `PWA_ICON_SVG` / `PWA_FAVICON_SVG`.
`theme_color` / `theme-color` = `#154c34`; manifest `background_color` = `#f3efe6`.
Bump the service-worker `CACHE` version when assets change so installed PWAs refresh.
